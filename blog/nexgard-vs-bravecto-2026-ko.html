NexGard vs Bravecto — Key Differences

NexGard is monthly dosing and protects against fleas, ticks, and mites. Bravecto provides 3 months of protection against fleas and most ticks. Bravecto's longer duration makes it more convenient; NexGard's monthly dosing gives more control if reactions occur.

  • NexGard: monthly dosing, fleas and ticks
  • Bravecto: 3-month dosing, broader tick coverage
  • Both are chewable oral treatments — no topical mess
  • Both require a veterinary prescription
