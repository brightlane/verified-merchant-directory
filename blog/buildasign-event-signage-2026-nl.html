Event Sign Types and When to Use Each

Different events need different sign types. Vinyl banners for outdoor events and grand entrances. Foam board for indoor table displays and directional signs. Retractable banners for trade show booths and presentations. Yard signs for outdoor directionals and parking guidance. Fabric banners for premium indoor events like weddings and galas.

  • Vinyl banners — outdoor events, grand openings, photo backdrops
  • Foam board signs — indoor directionals, table displays
  • Retractable banners — trade shows, corporate presentations
  • Yard signs — outdoor parking, event directionals
  • Fabric banners — weddings, galas, premium indoor events
  • Window clings — venue storefronts and glass displays
