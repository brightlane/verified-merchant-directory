What is a Tax Extension?

A tax extension allows taxpayers extra time to file their tax returns without facing immediate penalties. However, it’s essential to understand that an extension does not grant you more time to pay any taxes owed. The IRS typically permits a six-month extension (until October 15, 2025, for the 2024 tax year) but requires the extension request to be filed by the original due date of the return, usually April 15. For 2025, the IRS tax extension Form 4868 is the standard form used to request this extension.
