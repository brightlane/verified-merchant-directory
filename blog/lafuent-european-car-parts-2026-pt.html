Finding the Right Part Number for European Cars

European cars use consistent OEM part numbering systems. BMW uses a 9-digit part number. Mercedes uses a 9-digit A-number. Audi/VW uses a numeric format with dashes. Find your part number from the existing part, your owner's manual, or an online parts catalog. Searching by part number on Lafuent gives the most accurate match.
