Best PRINCE2 Certification Course 2026 — PM Training Review

Brightlane 편집부 · 게시일 2026-04-30 · 검토일 2026-04-30

PRINCE2 is the project management standard dominant in the UK, Europe, and Australia. If you work with international teams or plan to, it's increasingly valuable alongside or instead of PMP.

⚡ PRINCE2 Foundation is the entry level — achievable in 2-3 weeks of study. PRINCE2 Practitioner is the advanced level. PM Training covers both with their specialized curriculum.

PRINCE2 vs PMP — Which Should You Choose?

PMP is the global standard with strong recognition in North America. PRINCE2 is process-based and dominant in UK, Europe, Australia, and government sectors. Many project managers hold both.

  • PRINCE2 Foundation — entry level, 1 exam
  • PRINCE2 Practitioner — advanced level, 1 exam
  • Recognized globally, dominant in UK and Europe
  • No work experience requirement — unlike PMP
  • Process-based methodology — very structured
  • PM Training 98% pass rate

PRINCE2 Agile — The Newer Qualification

PRINCE2 Agile combines the structured PRINCE2 framework with agile delivery methods. It's increasingly popular for organizations transitioning from traditional to agile project management. PM Training also offers PRINCE2 Agile courses.

자주 묻는 질문

Is PRINCE2 harder than PMP?+
PRINCE2 Foundation is generally considered easier than PMP. PRINCE2 Practitioner is more challenging.
Is PRINCE2 recognized in the US?+
PRINCE2 is recognized globally but less dominant in the US than PMP. US government and defense sectors have stronger PRINCE2 presence.
How long does PRINCE2 certification take?+
Foundation can be achieved in 2-3 weeks. Combined Foundation + Practitioner programs run 5 days with PM Training.
